Hi, It's Axel-Loic AKANNI

I'm an Illustrator

Since I was a child, I have always been passionate about drawing, painting, art in general, and 2D animation. I made it my profession, and for more than 5 years I have been offering and selling my services in digital illustration and comic art… and for over 7 years in painting and fine arts. I also occasionally lead practical drawing and painting workshops. I am currently enrolled in a 2D animation incubation program at Sèmè City in partnership with Gobelins Paris, funded by the World Bank.

About Me

With a background in mechanical engineering and a year of studies in entrepreneurship in fine and digital arts at the University of Abomey-Calavi, I am currently pursuing a traditional 2D animation training program at Sèmè City in partnership with Gobelins Paris. Passionate about art since childhood, I have over 7 years of experience in painting and fine arts (portraits, watercolor, mixed media…) and, for the past 5 years, I have been working as a freelance illustrator and comic artist. I also occasionally lead painting and drawing workshops to share my skills and passion. Throughout my journey, I have developed strong expertise with tools such as Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint Pro, and TVPaint, as well as traditional techniques like painting, watercolor, and colored pencil drawing. My specialties include illustration, comics, character design, and background art, always with a detailed and colorful creative touch. My artistic approach is rooted in deeply understanding each subject before translating it into visuals, with the goal of conveying powerful emotions and addressing sensitive themes through an aesthetic that resonates and moves. I am currently working on a 2D animated film as part of my training at Sèmè City in collaboration with Gobelins Paris. I am available for illustration, comic art, and 2D animation projects, and can be reached via email or through my social media channels.
