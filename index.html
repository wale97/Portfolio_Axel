Hi, It's Axel-Loic AKANNI

I'm an Illustrator

Since I was a child, I have always been passionate about drawing, painting, art in general, and 2D animation. I made it my profession, and for more than 5 years I have been offering and selling my services in digital illustration and comic art… and for over 7 years in painting and fine arts. I also occasionally lead practical drawing and painting workshops. I am currently enrolled in a 2D animation incubation program at Sèmè City in partnership with Gobelins Paris, funded by the World Bank.

About Me

I am a passionate illustrator, comic artist, and 2D animator in training at Sèmè City in partnership with Gobelins Paris. With over 7 years of experience in painting and fine arts, and 5 years as a freelance illustrator and comic artist, I create detailed and colorful works that bring stories and emotions to life. Skilled in Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint Pro, TVPaint, and traditional techniques, I specialize in illustration, comics, character design, and background art. Currently working on a 2D animated film, I am open to collaborations in illustration, comics, and 2D animation.
